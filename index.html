Noon green is softened for this palette, desaturated from the heritage #64D8AE to #6BAE93 so it sits with the earth tones instead of jumping off them. It stays the live digital accent, used for state and the mark, never as a field. Terracotta and oak carry surface; future teal anchors the intelligent layer. Water is the colour of selection and progression: water gathered in the desert, the currency of ground covered. It marks the path travelled, the current position, a chosen answer, and lightens to #7C99B4 on dark surfaces. Royal purple is reserved for one voice only: the AI tutor, its messages, its speaking state, its glyph. It never appears anywhere else, so when you see purple, the AI is present.

03

Pattern system

الأنماط

Three pattern languages: Facet, Khatam and Pinboard. Each is shown in both voices, at scale, and in UI. Facet is taken directly from the classroom walls: one faceted lattice in two intensities, dunes (orange) and plaster (light). Abstract, the desert as geometry, from a wall mural down to a 14px app texture.
