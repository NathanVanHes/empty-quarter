Noon green is softened for this palette — desaturated from the heritage #64D8AE to #6BAE93 so it sits with the earth tones instead of jumping off them. It stays the live digital accent — used for state and the mark, never as a field. Terracotta and oak carry surface; future teal anchors the intelligent layer. Royal purple is reserved for one voice only: the AI tutor — its messages, its speaking state, its glyph. It never appears anywhere else, so when you see purple, the AI is present.

03

Pattern system

الأنماط

Two working pattern languages — Facet and Mesh — with the khatam star as a supporting ornament tile. Each is abstract, the desert as geometry, and each tiles seamlessly from a wall mural down to a 14px app texture.

01

Facet

the canyon, broken to planes الأوجه
Facet grid · shaded to the lattice
Strata · sediment — seamless tile, shown repeating
Khatam · the one ornament — signage, watermarks, dividers

Where it lives: hero brand moments, wall murals, section covers, posters and splash. The bold, terracotta-led voice.
Facet · at scale

The terrain

the desert, built from planes — pure pattern التضاريس

The mural is pattern, nothing more — dunes built from planes, running edge to edge. Nothing sits on top of it. It works as a wall, a hero band, a cover; the journey lives in its own UI layer below.

05

In product

في التطبيق
